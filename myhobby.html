MY HOBBY ❤

One fact you must know about me is that i really love to participate in sport.

I love to Cycling 🚲

Cycling has become an integral part of my life. Whenever I have free time, you can find me pedaling through the streets, exploring new paths, and embracing the freedom that cycling brings. It's a hobby that keeps me active, uplifts my spirits, and allows me to appreciate the world around me in a unique way.

I love to play netball 🏀

I have been playing netball since I was in primary school. Playing netball from a young age creates a sense of familiarity and a strong connection with the sport. Consistent involvement over the years has allowed me to develop a deep bond and attachment to netball, making it part of my life and identity.

I love to go hiking 🏖

Hiking is a fantastic way to engage in physical activity and improve my overall fitness. Walking or climbing on trails, navigating uneven terrain, and encountering various challenges along the way contribute to cardiovascular health, muscle strength, and endurance. Near my house, there is a renowned hill called Bukit Belacan that is perfect for hiking. I always hike this hill.
